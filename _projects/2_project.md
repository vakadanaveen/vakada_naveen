A comprehensive chatbot system designed to handle a wide range of audio-related queries by integrating multiple specialized audio processing models.

The system uses an **intent classifier** to route queries to expert models such as:
- Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)
- Audio event detection
- Speaker identification
- Music understanding

By decomposing audio understanding into specialized sub-tasks and routing intelligently, the system achieves robust performance across diverse query types without requiring a single monolithic model.

**Published:** EMNLP 2025  
**Author:** Naveen Vakada et al.

**MS Thesis project at IIT Madras** (2022–2023), supervised by Prof. C. Chandra Sekhar.

Visual paragraph generation involves producing a descriptive, coherent multi-sentence paragraph from a single input image — going beyond single-sentence captioning to capture the full scene narrative.

**Key contributions:**
- Developed transformer-based models that do not rely on region-level supervision (bounding box annotations)
- Introduced novel semantic post-processing to improve coherence across sentences
- Achieved state-of-the-art performance on the **METEOR metric (19.2)** on the Visual Genome paragraph dataset

Published at **ACIVS 2023** (Lecture Notes in Computer Science, Vol. 14124, Springer).

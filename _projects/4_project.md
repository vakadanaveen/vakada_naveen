Image captioning in low-resource languages faces a fundamental data scarcity problem. This project addresses it by leveraging high-resource English captions as a bridge to improve caption quality in Hindi and German.

**Approach:**
- Transfer learning from English-pretrained vision-language models
- Cross-lingual alignment strategies to adapt captions to target languages
- Validated on **Hindi-Flickr8k** and **Multi-30k German** datasets

**Published:** CVIP 2024, Communications in Computer and Information Science, Vol. 2477, pp. 25–38, Springer.  
**Authors:** Naveen Vakada, C. Sekhar Chandra

During my tenure as **Senior Machine Learning Researcher at Qualcomm R&D** (2024–2026), I led several high-impact research projects in multimodal AI:

**Projects:**
- **Multimodal LLMs:** Extending large language models to jointly understand video, audio, and on-device sensor data
- **Audio Conversational Systems:** End-to-end dialogue systems that reason over audio content
- **Video Localization:** Temporal grounding of natural language queries in long video streams
- **RAG for Multimodal Understanding:** Efficient evidence retrieval across modalities

**Output:** 8 patents filed — 4 US applications (2025) and 4 Indian applications (2024) covering audio AI, multimodal reasoning, and self-consistency in LLMs.

**Awards:** Runner-up, SparQ Hackathon (2025); Winner, Qualcomm Multimedia Whitepaper Contest (2024).
